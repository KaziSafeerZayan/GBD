Our Aim

Our intention is to become a successful team and create things that will help other people.


The Anti Load Shedding Machine

We use devices for many purposes in our life. We also need to charge them. That is where the Anti Load Shedding Machine comes in to play. It helps to charge the devices without using any types of fossil fuel or any non-renewable resources. It helps to reduce climate change and charge our devices at the same time. This is a very important project for us.
